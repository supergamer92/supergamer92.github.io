⚔ HOW TO PLAY ⚔

BASICS
• Survive endless waves — no finish line!
• Your character auto-shoots at enemies
• Collect XP orbs to level up & pick upgrades
• A boss appears every 5th wave
• Kill combos boost your XP drops
ENEMIES
• GRUNT/RUNNER/TANK — standard foes
• EXPLODER — detonates near you!
• NECROMANCER — restores enemy HP
• BOMBER — visible fuse, erupts on death
• TELEPORTER — blinks behind you
• JUGGERNAUT — charges at full speed
UPGRADES & CHESTS
• COMMON — minor stat boost
• RARE — strong improvement
• EPIC — powerful build-defining effects
• Stats use diminishing returns — diversify!
• Boss chests give 9 choices · Reroll costs 💰
TIPS & SYSTEMS
• Use SPACE ability — cooldowns are short!
• 🔥 RAMPAGE: 10 kills in 6s = 1.5× dmg for 8s
• 👑 ELITE enemies are harder, give 2.5× XP
• Watch the minimap (top-right) for threats
• TAB to view full stats & item list mid-run
